Health Program

Here in the health programs we do many hands on activites such as cpr, pig dissections, rat dissections, and rabbit dissections. We use these dissections to learn more about the human body and it's functions.

This is a photo of the students practicing cpr on cpr manikins. At the end of their cpr practices at clarkson they get a cpr certificate which allows the students to perform cpr in an emergency.


Requirements

There are some conditions that have to be met to join the health academy. An requirement is that you have to have a 3.0 Gpa or teacher recomendation to be accepted. While in the health academy you will be required to do the science fair as a triple summative. The resources for the science fair will be payed for you as long as it stays in the given budget otherwise you will have to pay for it.
